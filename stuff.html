Making a physics simulation is not as easy as I thought! I spent months working on the code, just so I could get something functional. I decided I wanted to share my "hard" work and guide you through how my code works.
Lets begin with the most basic things, the way our particle moves. Our particles have X and Y coordonates. I express them in the form of vectors.

this.Update=function(){
   this.Pos=this.NextPos();
   this.Vel.set(Vadd(this.Vel,this.Acc));
   this.Acc.set(this.Grav);
}
this.NextPos=function(){
  return Vadd(this.Pos,Vadd(this.Vel,Vmult(this.Acc,0.5)));
}
