Welcome to the HKIS Economics Club website!

Why are we here?
    We operate to better student's journey in economics, allowing them to learn faster and understand deeper, advancing their interest in economics. This is done by offering a comprehensive, engaging, and interactive learning service in economics and financial literacy.

In this site, you will find economic resources for the AP Micro and Macro Economics courses, and for anyone interested in furthering their understanding of econ and their financial literacy. This site contains-
  • In depth study guides
  • Test practice and unit review materials
  • Interactive explanations of fundamental economics concepts
  • Economics games and quizzes
If you are an HKIS student, join our club discord server for review sessions, 1 on 1 tutor sessions, and more information.
